What We Are

A venture studio that builds from first principles.

Tricycle Labz is not an agency, consultancy, or accelerator. We are a product-focused venture studio that rapidly designs, validates, and launches AI-native products ... especially in emotional wellness, family life, education, and behaviour change.

Every product begins with a real human need, is shaped by scientific research, and is built with the speed and discipline of a startup that ships.

Why We Exist

Technology should support human flourishing ... not just capture attention.

Most consumer technology is optimised for engagement, not development. We believe AI creates an opportunity to invert that ... to build products that genuinely help people grow, reflect, and connect.

Our work is grounded in neuroscience, psychology, and behavioural science. Not as marketing language, but as the foundation for every product decision we make.
