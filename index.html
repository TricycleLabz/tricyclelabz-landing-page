What We Are

Consumer technology for human psychological flourishing.

Tricycle Labz is a venture studio building AI-native products that help everyday people increase self-awareness, agency, and productivity. We take a high-iteration, low-ego, deeply customer-centred approach to building.

Rather than guessing what people want, we test lightweight concepts early through landing pages, messaging, and simple demos ... then follow real demand signals. We are intentionally fully bootstrapped, with no venture capital, to ensure each venture is shaped by genuine customer demand rather than artificial momentum.

Origin

Three brothers. A desert. A decision to leave something better.

The inspiration for Tricycle Labz began in the deserts of Joshua Tree, during a moment of unusual clarity. Removed from our day-to-day environments, we found ourselves recognising a clear convergence ... across the problems we were drawn to, the skills we had developed over time, and the stage of life we were each entering.

What became evident was not just a shared curiosity, but a shared responsibility: an opportunity to build something meaningful that could contribute to helping people better understand themselves, relate to one another, and dramatically improve their day-to-day capabilities. The moment also carried a personal dimension ... an intention to create something we could stand behind for our children and the next generation.

Values

Not aspirational. Operational.

Make the invisible visible.

We are drawn to the problems people cannot quite name but feel every day. The gap between who someone intends to be and how they actually show up. We build tools that bring those things into focus, because clarity is a form of care.

Follow the problem, not the category.

We build across emotional development, relationships, self-knowledge, and creativity because we kept finding the same thing in each: people are trying to do better, and the tools they have are not good enough. We did not choose a vertical. We chose a conviction.

Build for the next generation.

Every decision runs through a simple filter: does this leave something better for the people coming after us? Short-term wins that compromise long-term integrity are not wins.

Rigour earns the right to be bold.

The Scientist demands evidence before we claim anything. The Innovator refuses to wait for permission. The Strategist knows when to listen to each. We do not use rigour as an excuse to move slowly, or boldness as an excuse to skip the work.

How We Work

High iteration. Low ego. Real signal.

01

02

03

04
